Responsive

Headings shrink at the sm breakpoint (640px): the standard variant goes text-3xl → sm:text-4xl, the blog-post variant goes text-3xl → sm:text-5xl. Section padding and bottom margins also halve on mobile (pt-10 sm:pt-20, mb-6 sm:mb-8). Line-height softens from a tight leading-[1.05] down to leading-tight on mobile so wrapped lines don't crash into each other at smaller font sizes.

<p class="text-xs uppercase tracking-[0.2em]
   text-muted mb-3">Work</p>
<h1 class="display
   text-3xl sm:text-4xl
   font-semibold
   leading-tight sm:leading-[1.1]
   mb-4">
  Where I&rsquo;ve worked.
</h1>
<p class="text-muted mb-6 sm:mb-8
   max-w-2xl leading-relaxed">
  A chronological summary. …
</p>

The hero variant on the home page also stacks — photo on top, text below — at sub-sm widths. Photo disc shrinks from w-40 h-40 to w-24 h-24.
